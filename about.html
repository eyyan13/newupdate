Our Story

AKBAR AND ASSOCIATES, now in the fifth decade of pioneer engineering design and operation, was founded by Mr. AKBAR GUL in 1976.

As a Consulting Engineering firm, Akbar and Associates practices in the planning, design and cost control of construction projects all over Pakistan and overseas. We have played a vital role and had an enormous influence on the face of industrialization in Pakistan. With unparalleled expertise in a wide spectrum of diverse industrial projects, we also take pride in our achievements through historical monuments, iconic high-rise buildings, hospitals, bridges and much more, throughout the country.
